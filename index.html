Cat Poetry - tiny reflections on my girlfriend's roommate's cats

Isa is a beautiful and dangerous cat. She will draw you in with her beauty and then bite you. Like a razor leaf on a tree in the fall.

Teya is a happy beast. Cookie monster is a good comparison. They both remind us sugar is sweet, but not beautiful.

Cats are cats. Humans are humans...

A cat poem in list form:

  • There is fur
  • There are teeth
  • And many a whisker
  • May your paws not pause
    1. when the enemy comes
    2. when the bird does fly
    3. when it's time to run
